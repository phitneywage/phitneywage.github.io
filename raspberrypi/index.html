A collection of raspberry pi creations from the brain of Steven C.

In the beginning...

While spinning on my spin bike one afternoon, I started to wonder how effeient my exercise regimen really was. How many RPM's? How quickly is my heart rate climbing and dropping and how can I keep it in the fat burning zone?

So Now What?

As an avid tinkering soul, I stared building my own heartrate monitor for my basic spin bike. As this led to more ideas and projects, my excitement grew. I might not be the only one interested in this data.
