What is Pi?

Can I eat the raspberry pi? Unfortunately no. This pi is a low cost, credit card sized computer that can be transformed into a variety of electronic projects. You can enhance your lifestyle or simply build games to entertain. The projects are essentially limitless


A few ideas

My Pi interest started on my spin bike. I wanted an accurate heart rate monitor that would not only help me track my bpm but also graph out for me the efficiency of each work out. The Pi is not limited to this, you can:

  • Create an interactive mirror with widgets of your choice
  • Recreate old school gaming systems
  • Build interactive music systems
  • Make any old household item interactive!
