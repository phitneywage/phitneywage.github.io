What is Pi?

Can I eat te raspberry pi? Unfortunately no. This pi is a low cost, credit card sized computer that can be transformed into a variety of electronic projects. You can enhance your lifestyleor simply build games to entertain. The projects are essentially limitless.

Why Pi?

My pi interest started while riding my spin bike. I wanted an accurate heart rate monitor that would not only track my bpm, but would also graph out the efficiency of each workout.

A few ideas are:

  • Create an interactive mirror with widgets of your choice
  • Recreate old school gaming systems
  • Build interactive music systems
  • Make any old household item interactive!
